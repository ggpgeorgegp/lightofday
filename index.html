The message determines the medium.

Sometimes a film. Sometimes a photograph. Sometimes a conversation.

We bring stories into the Light.

Light of Day is an independent storytelling practice based in Bengaluru.

For over two decades, we've worked with organisations, communities and institutions to uncover stories worth telling — through research, writing, photography and film.

Every assignment begins the same way. With curiosity.
